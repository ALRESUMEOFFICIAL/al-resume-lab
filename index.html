Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ATS and why does it matter?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by employers to screen candidates automatically before a human recruiter reads the CV. Over 90% of large companies use ATS.

Are the resumes optimized for Gulf & Middle East jobs?

Yes, AL Resume Lab includes localized ATS rules tailored for markets in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, as well as Western remote job requirements.

Can I edit my resume after generating it?

Absolutely! You can customize sections, edit descriptions, adjust formatting, and re-run ATS checks anytime.
